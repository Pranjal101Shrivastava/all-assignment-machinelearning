=== BLENDED POEM ===
Original: 'Hope is the thing with feathers' by Emily Dickinson
Nouns replaced with most similar nouns from Robert Frost's vocabulary
========================================

Hope is the one with woods –
That birches in the world –
And sings the boy without the piece –
And never stops – at all –

And savage – in the Gale – is heard –
And swell must be the storm –
That could abash the little Bird
That kept so many warm –

I've heard it in the chillest land –
And on the strangest Sea –
Yet – never – in Stiffness,
It asked a stir – of me.
